To run:
Install Python 3!
And Pyglet!

Downloads: (Direct Links) (These are the ones i have)
https://www.python.org/ftp/python/3.4.1/python-3.4.1.msi
https://bitbucket.org/pyglet/pyglet/downloads/pyglet-1.2.4.zip

After installing Python, put the "pyglet" folder in this directory:
C:\Python34\Lib\site-packages
P.S. Not the "pyglet-1.2.4" folder. the "pyglet" one inside it. lol

Controls:
WSAD: Move Player
KEY E: Toggle Mouse
SPACE: Fly Up
LSHIFT: Fly Down
MOUSE LEFT: Destroy
MOUSE RIGHT: Place

What you can learn from these scripts:

Minecraft 1 (Basic Start):
   A basic block engine!

Minecraft 2 (First Water):
   Added animated water blocks!

Minecraft 3 (Perlin Noise):
   Terrain Generation!

Minecraft 4 (Trees and Grass):
   Self Explanatory here! :)

Minecraft 5 (Chunks and Swimming):
   Load world in a given chunk size!
   And swimming in water!
